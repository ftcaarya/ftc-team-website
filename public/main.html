About Circuit Breakers

Our Mission

Circuit Breakers is a passionate team of high school students dedicated to exploring the frontiers of robotics and engineering. Our mission is to develop innovative solutions to complex problems while fostering a culture of teamwork, creativity, and continuous learning. All while promoting our love for first to others.

We believe in pushing boundaries, breaking barriers, and creating innovative technological solutions to benefit the entire FIRST community.

Our Values

  • Innovation: Constantly seeking creative solutions to complex challenges
  • Collaboration: Working together to leverage our diverse skills and perspectives
  • Resilience: Embracing failures as learning opportunities
  • Excellence: Striving for the highest quality in everything we do
  • Inclusion: Welcoming and valuing diversity in our team

Our Robot

Circuit Breaker X-1

Meet our latest robot design, engineered for precision, speed, and versatility. The X-1 represents the culmination of months of iterative design, testing, and refinement.

  • Control System: Advanced REV Robotics control hub with custom firmware
  • Drive Train: Mecanum wheel configuration for omnidirectional mobility
  • Manipulator: Dual-action gripper with precision placement system
  • Vision System: Computer vision with TensorFlow for game element detection
  • Autonomous: Multiple pathfinding algorithms with dynamic adaptation

Design Process

Concept & Ideation

Initial brainstorming and strategy analysis to determine optimal robot capabilities for the season's challenge.

Prototyping

Rapid development of multiple subsystem prototypes to test feasibility and performance.

Integration & Testing

Combining successful subsystems and iteratively testing complete robot performance.

Refinement

Optimizing mechanisms, code, and operation based on real-world testing results.

Competition Ready

Final adjustments, driver practice, and documentation completion for competition.
