OUR MISSION

Circuit Breakers is a passionate team of high school students dedicated to exploring the frontiers of robotics and engineering. Our mission is to develop innovative solutions to complex problems while fostering a culture of teamwork, creativity, and continuous learning.

We believe in pushing boundaries, breaking barriers, and creating innovative technological solutions to benefit the entire FIRST community.
